University Student

Hi, I’m Saw Min — an aspiring developer passionate about using technology to make communication easier and improve people’s lives. I’m especially interested in creating tools that break down language barriers and exploring how AI can support safer and higher-quality healthcare. I’m constantly working to strengthen my skills in programming, system design, and AI. I like learning independently, digging into resources, and experimenting with ideas until they become something real. When I get stuck, I research, test, and explore until I find a solution. I want to grow into a developer who creates tools that genuinely help people around the world.
